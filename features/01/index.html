Features

Transparent shipment tracking

To simplify tracking and allow overall picture we split more complex shipments to smaller traceable phases. Each phase (pick up, main transport, delivery…) is easily managed by dispatcher.

Simple planning

Assign shipment to driver and let it appear in his mobile application Tracedo with full details for pick up and delivery.

Precise tracking

Time and place of each milestone such as pick up, delivery etc. confirmed by driver in app will be automatically saved.


Tracking of actual position of shipment

Mobile application automatically log GPS coordinates during transportation. Dispatcher and customer can view actual position of cargo or view its routing on the map.

Communication

System offers easy communication during transportation by sharing details with driver.

Smooth integration with external systems

Tracedo allows ability to integrate with your information system and automatically transfer the orders and share the status information.

How it works

Mobile

Driver needs the mobile app (Android) only. Dispatcher uses web portal to distribute freight among drivers. Driver views his freight in mobile app.

Online

Driver confirms pick up and delivery in mobile app. Mobile app sends position of freight during the transportation. Dispatcher and drive communicate online.

Automatically

Tracedo notifies dispatchers about cargo status. Customers have status information and shipment details integrated in their information systems.
